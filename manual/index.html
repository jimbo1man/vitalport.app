User Manual

This guide will grow with the app. For now, it covers what VitalPort does and where to get help. Open the app to configure exports, destinations, and schedules.

Overview

VitalPort reads Apple Health data on your device and lets you generate JSON or CSV and send it to endpoints you define. No account is required in the app.

Privacy

Health data stays on your device unless you export or send it. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Questions
