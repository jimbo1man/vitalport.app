Privacy Policy

VitalPort does not collect, store, or transmit personal data by default.

All health data accessed by VitalPort comes from Apple Health and remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to export it or send it to a destination you configure.

VitalPort does not include analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party data collection.

If you choose to send data to an external endpoint, that data is transmitted only to the destination you specify.

VitalPort uses Apple-provided encryption such as HTTPS/TLS for network communication.

For questions, feedback, or privacy requests, use the App Store support link or contact the developer through the support information listed on the App Store.
